Services

Press Ons

A press-on nail extension is a pre-made, artificial nail tip made of plastic (like ABS) that you adhere directly onto your natural nail using glue or adhesive tabs, offering a quick, temporary, and DIY way to get instant length and style without a lengthy salon visit, unlike traditional extensions

Starting at ₹499/ ₹299/-

Acrylic Extensions

Acrylic nail extensions are a popular and versatile nail enhancement technique used to create longer, stronger, and beautifully sculpted nails. This method involves a combination of liquid monomer and powder polymer that forms a sturdy layer over your natural nails or nail tips.

Starting at ₹1299/ ₹999/-

Temporary Extensions

Gel nail extensions are artificial nail enhancements that add length and strength using a flexible gel formula, applied over the natural nail (often with a tip or form) and hardened (cured) under a UV or LED lamp, resulting in a glossy, natural-looking, and lightweight finish that's an alternative to acrylics

Starting at ₹799/ ₹599/-
